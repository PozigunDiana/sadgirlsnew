The Story
There is a place in this damn city, a shelter for the dark souls. You can find a companion here and have something to drink. Late night, you walk in the street, it’s so cold and rainy...

Your eyes, almost blinded by the heavy rain, suddenly spotted a pale shimmering light on the other side of the road.

What is there? A bar?... Narrowed eyes can hardly read... Neon sign says “Sad Girls Bar”...

You step in. In a seconds you’re warmly embraced by the light scent of smoke mixed with perfume… You take off your wet leather jacket... Do you feel it? Something so familiar, but what is this... patchouli or oud, or…

You look around, slowly. Walls and floor are illuminated by amber light from small table lamps. You slowly dissolve, resting in peaceful and comfy atmosphere.

Few silhouettes are here and there. When you come closely to the bar desk, there is a girl.

Sad girl with eyes wide shut. Does her face seem familiar to you?

Something starts to rushing inside your chest... And suddenly you hear her words, that are almost silent “Hmm... you could sit next to me”.
